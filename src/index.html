Why I design

Before stepping into product design, I spent nearly a decade working in various business support roles. Looking back, I now realise I was already shaping customer experiences.
My real introduction to UI/UX design came in 2020, when I took a role as a Requirements Engineer for a car dealership app. That’s when I first noticed the Product Design team. They weren’t just documenting features, they were defining the user experience and shaping the product from the ground up.

That sparked something in me. I didn’t want to just document a product, I wanted to design it. Providing intuitive, user-centered, meaningful experiences.

Designing for enterprise

I focus on enterprise web design. In 2022, I joined a global leader in enterprise automation.

Working in a large company came with a specific set of challenges:

  • Complexity in design, product and platform.
  • Cross-team collaboration.
  • Customer diversity & experience gaps.
  • Decision-making and governance.
  • Unified, scalable design system.

The road ahead

The road ahead is about learning, refining my craft, and staying curious.
As I continue to grow as a product designer, I’m focused on deepening my impact, building not just usable interfaces, but thoughtful, scalable solutions that genuinely improve people’s lives.
I want to keep pushing beyond the surface, collaborating closely with users, engineers, and stakeholders to solve the right problems.

Showcases

Monitoring dashboards

Help users interpret trends, detect issues and make informed decisions quickly by consolidateing and presenting key data in a single, accessible interface.

Keywords: data visualization, real-time metrics, UX clarity, performance monitoring, alerting, user-centric design

Configuration wizards

Reducing cognitive load and user error by simplifying complex setup flows and unifying disconnected steps into a guided, cohesive process.

Keywords: progressive disclosure, step-by-step flows, onboarding, setup automation, user guidance, task simplification

Contextual experiences

Maintaining context and minimizing disruptions. by enabling users to dive into specific content or make detailed edits without losing their place.

Keywords: live previews, side panels, embedded workflows, inline editing, form-in-form, contextual UI, multitasking support

Data grids

Enhancing grid usability and introducing powerful filtering, sorting, and search options, by improveing data discoverability and manipulation tailored for both novice and advanced users.

Keywords: data tables, smart filtering, multi-criteria sorting, inline controls, search UX, column customization, user productivity

Mobile responsiveness

How to adapt complex desktop web patterns, such as multi-layer tab navigation and dense interfaces for mobile screens, ensuring usability, accessibility, and performance across devices.

Keywords: responsive design, mobile-first, adaptive UI, collapsible navigation, gesture support, viewport optimization, usability on small screens
